## Key Strategic Insight

**Ideas 2 and 3 can be built and sold in parallel** because they target completely different audiences (field service tradespeople vs. boutique law firms). The distribution channels, messaging, and product stacks don't overlap. This means we can potentially pursue both simultaneously within the first month.

**Idea 1 is the riskiest** not because the market is bad (it's great), but because the feedback loop is too slow. SEO takes 3‚Äì6 months to show results, and our framework explicitly prioritizes fast time-to-revenue. If a plumber pays $199/mo for 3 months and sees nothing, they cancel. This creates a structural churn problem that's hard to solve without changing the product's fundamental value proposition (e.g., adding instant-value features like review management or GBP optimization that show results faster).
