## Methodology

1. **Deep dives** were completed for 32 of the 94 long-listed ideas by independent AI agents.
2. **Peer review** was performed by 4 independent agents, producing 128 idea-specific feedback files and 4 cross-cutting summaries.
3. **PM synthesis** consolidated all feedback, adjusted framework scores, triaged ideas, and resolved redundancies.
4. **Analysis revisions** were applied to top-priority ideas (`analysis.md` updated in place).
5. **Re-ranking** reflects the post-review adjusted scores below.

The **individual `analysis.md`** in each idea folder is the single source of truth. This ranking is an index ‚Äî it tells you which analysis to open first.
